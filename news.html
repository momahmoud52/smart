Project Milestone

Completion of The Platinum Tower Foundation Phase

SMART VISION successfully completed the heavy structural foundation of the new Platinum Tower ahead of schedule, utilizing advanced seismic engineering techniques.

SMART VISION successfully completed the heavy structural foundation of the new Platinum Tower ahead of schedule, utilizing advanced seismic engineering techniques. This phase involved pouring over 15,000 cubic meters of high-strength concrete continuously for 48 hours.

Engineering Challenges Overcome

The ground conditions at the site presented unique challenges, requiring deep piling reaching up to 60 meters below ground level. Our structural engineering team collaborated seamlessly with geospatial analysts to ensure zero deviation during the execution.

With this foundation complete, the tower is poised to rise at an estimated rate of one floor every six days, staying well within the timeline projected for Q4 2028.
